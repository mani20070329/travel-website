Must-See Places

Uluwatu Temple

Perched on a cliff, Uluwatu Temple offers breathtaking sunset views and cultural performances. Be sure to catch the Kecak fire dance in the evening for a memorable experience.

Rice Terraces of Tegallalang

The stunning rice terraces are not only a feast for the eyes but also a great place for a leisurely walk. Enjoy the fresh air and take in the lush greenery surrounding you.

Monkey Forest in Ubud

Home to hundreds of playful monkeys, the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary is a great spot to observe these lively creatures. Just be cautious with your belongings!

Kuta Beach

Famous for its vibrant nightlife and surfing opportunities, Kuta Beach is a must-visit. Spend your day soaking up the sun and your evening enjoying local beach clubs.

Mount Batur

For adventure seekers, hiking Mount Batur for sunrise is an unforgettable experience. The breathtaking views from the summit make the early wake-up call worth it.
